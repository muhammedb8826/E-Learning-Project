CODING WITH M.B
GLOBAL SUMMIT 2023
The Coding with MB brings together the community of experts, and activists who comprise the Coding with M.b affiliate network in a different country every two years

This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the Coding with MB mission. the cause o the commons, free culture and open knowledge. So if you're active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge - free software advocates, Wikipedians, Open knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant commons together.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about Coding with MB Global Summit 2023!
mbsummit2023@mbethiopia.org
